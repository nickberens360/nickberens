Jekyll kind of sort of supports post tags. You have to fill in the blanks if want to be able to have tags in posts and link to the corresponding tag archive page.

Fortunately Charlie Park wrote a great article on how to create a plugin on how to accomplish this. The only problem is that he left out how to display the tags on the post itself and link it to the archive page.

I won't go through steps to create the plugin, you can read Charlie's article for that. Here is the missing line you will need to add to your posts template:

{% for tag in page.tags %}<a class="tag-link" href="{{ site.url }}/tag/{{ tag }}">{{ tag }}</a>{% endfor %}

Simple, sort of.
